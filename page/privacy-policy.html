Privacy Policy

Meet our certified specialists, discover their areas of expertise, and choose the one who aligns perfectly with your goals—from building strength to practicing mindful yoga.

At Fitova Gym, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we gather, utilize, and protect your data when you access our website, mobile app, or visit our gym locations.

  1. Information We Collect

    • Personal Information

    • Name, email, phone number, date of birth, and address.

    • Payment Details

    • Billing address and credit/debit card information (securely processed through third-party payment gateways).

  2. How We Use Your Information

    • Schedule classes, personal training sessions, and gym access.

    • Enhance our services and tailor your fitness experience.

    • Send you important updates, promotions, and fitness tips.

    • Address your inquiries and provide customer support.

  3. How We Protect Your Information

    • Secure servers and encrypted storage solutions.

    • Restricted access to personal data by authorized personnel only.

    • Regular system monitoring and updates to avert breaches.


  4. Sharing Your Information

    • Trusted third-party service providers (such as payment processors and booking platforms) under strict confidentiality agreements.

    • Legal authorities when required by law.

  5. Cookies and Tracking

    We utilize cookies to:

    • Enhance website functionality and user experience.

    • Analyze site traffic and app performance.


      You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.
